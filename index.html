Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

We are introducing a Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) system to provide you with enhanced account protection of your.

This system has been integrated to align with the highest security standards and to offer you a robust defence against unauthorised access. 2FA adds an extra layer of security on top of your regular login credentials. This two-step authentication process ensures that only you can access your account, even if your password is compromised.
